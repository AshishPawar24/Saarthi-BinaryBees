Frequently Asked Questions

How are caregivers vetted?

All caregivers on our platform undergo a rigorous screening process that includes background checks, credential verification, reference checks, and in-person interviews. We verify all certifications and professional licenses to ensure they are current and valid.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with my caregiver?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your caregiver, contact us within the first two weeks and we'll help you find a replacement at no additional cost. Our goal is to ensure a perfect match between caregivers and clients.

Can I request a specific language or cultural background?

Absolutely! Our search filters allow you to specify language preferences and cultural backgrounds. We understand that communication and cultural compatibility are essential aspects of the caregiver-client relationship.

How quickly can I find a caregiver?

For standard care needs, most clients find and begin working with a caregiver within 2-5 days. For urgent needs, our expedited matching service can connect you with qualified caregivers within 24 hours. Emergency placements are also available.

Are caregivers insured?

Yes, all caregivers on our platform are covered by our liability insurance policy. Additionally, independent caregivers may carry their own insurance, and agency-employed caregivers are covered by their agency's insurance policies.
